Title: Privacy Policy for Kreee Analytics Effective Date: April 14, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how Kreee Analytics ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our internal analytics tool. Information We Collect: We only collect public metrics and statistics from your TikTok account via the TikTok API, including view counts, like counts, and share counts of your videos. How We Use Information: The data collected is used solely for personal content analysis and performance tracking through our automated workflows (n8n). Data Storage: All data is stored locally on the user's infrastructure (PostgreSQL/Google Sheets) and is not shared with or sold to any third parties. Data Security: We implement industry-standard security measures, including N-tier architecture and secure credential management, to protect your access tokens.
